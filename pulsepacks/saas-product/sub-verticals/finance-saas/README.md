# SaaS Finance Sub-Vertical

Focuses on Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) bridges, expansion/churn dynamics, cohort unit economics (LTV:CAC), and financial payback speed.

## Focus Areas

- **Revenue Bridge:** Decomposing ARR movements into New, Expansion, Contraction, and Churn components to evaluate recurring revenue health.
- **Capital Efficiency:** Analyzing LTV:CAC ratios and CAC payback periods to direct sales and marketing spend efficiently.
- **Retention Health:** Auditing Net Revenue Retention (NRR) to measure product expansion and customer success performance.

The following KPIs track subscription cohort health, capital acquisition efficiency, and net recurring revenue expansion in B2B/B2C SaaS models.

## NRR %

- **Name:** Net Revenue Retention %
- **Definition:** Measures subscription cohort health by tracking the expansion, contraction, and churn impacts of a starting revenue base.
- **Formula:** NRR % = (Starting ARR + Expansion ARR - Contraction ARR - Churned ARR) / (Starting ARR) x 100
- **Direction:** higher-is-better
- **Thresholds:**
  - `good`: >= 115
  - `watch`: 100 - 114.9
  - `risk`: < 100
- **Authoritative Source ID:** `SAAS-F02`
- **Typical Refresh Cadence:** Monthly
- **Glossary cross-reference:** NRR

## LTV:CAC Ratio

- **Name:** LTV:CAC Ratio
- **Definition:** The ratio of Customer Lifetime Value to Customer Acquisition Cost, evaluating the return on acquisition spend.
- **Formula:** LTV:CAC = (Customer Lifetime Value) / (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **Direction:** higher-is-better
- **Thresholds:**
  - `good`: >= 3
  - `watch`: 2.0 - 2.99
  - `risk`: < 2.0
- **Authoritative Source ID:** `SAAS-F01`
- **Typical Refresh Cadence:** Monthly
- **Glossary cross-reference:** LTV, CAC

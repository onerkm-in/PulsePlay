The following KPI tracks data center energy efficiency and power usage overheads for SaaS hosting infrastructure.

## Cloud PUE

- **Name:** Cloud Power Usage Effectiveness
- **Definition:** The ratio of total facility energy to IT equipment energy, measuring data center power efficiency.
- **Formula:** Cloud PUE = Total Facility Energy / IT Equipment Energy
- **Direction:** lower-is-better
- **Thresholds:**
  - `good`: <= 1.2
  - `watch`: 1.21 - 1.5
  - `risk`: > 1.5
- **Authoritative Source ID:** `ESG-S01`
- **Typical Refresh Cadence:** Daily
- **Glossary cross-reference:** Cloud compute PUE

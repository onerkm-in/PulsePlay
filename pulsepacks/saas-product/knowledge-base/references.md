# SaaS & Digital Product Source Register

This register catalogues standard bibliography references used in SaaS & Digital Products presets.

## References

### `SAAS-F01`
- **Publisher/Body:** SaaS Metrics Standards Board
- **Title:** Subscription Unit Economics & CAC Guide
- **URL:** https://www.saasmetricsboard.org/standards/unit-economics
- **Scope:** Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Customer Lifetime Value (LTV), and CAC Payback formulas
- **Credibility Tier:** `tier-1-standard`

### `SAAS-F02`
- **Publisher/Body:** KeyBanc Capital Markets
- **Title:** Annual SaaS Survey and Valuation Framework
- **URL:** https://www.keybanc.com/en/insights/saas-survey.html
- **Scope:** Net Revenue Retention (NRR), ARR bridges, and growth benchmarks
- **Credibility Tier:** `tier-4-industry-analysis`

### `ESG-S01`
- **Publisher/Body:** Green Software Foundation
- **Title:** Software Carbon Intensity (SCI) Standard
- **URL:** https://grnsft.org/sci-specification
- **Scope:** Cloud infrastructure Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) and green computing indicators
- **Credibility Tier:** `tier-1-standard`

# SaaS & Digital Product Glossary

Alphabetical index of standard SaaS metrics and cloud ESG terminology.

## Glossary

- **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue):** The annualized value of recurring subscription fees from active customers. Source: `SAAS-F01`.
- **Cloud compute PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness):** Power Usage Effectiveness measuring the energy efficiency of the data center facility hosting server workloads. Calculated as Total Facility Energy divided by IT Equipment Energy. Source: `ESG-S01`.
- **NRR (Net Revenue Retention):** The percentage of recurring revenue retained from an existing cohort of customers over a specified period, taking into account expansion, contraction, and churn. Source: `SAAS-F02`.
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** The total sales and marketing spend divided by the number of new customers acquired during a given period. Source: `SAAS-F01`.
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** The projected total net revenue generated by a customer over the duration of their relationship with the SaaS business. Source: `SAAS-F01`.

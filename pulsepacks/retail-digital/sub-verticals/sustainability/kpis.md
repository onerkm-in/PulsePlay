The following KPI tracks environmental impact and circular economy transition progress for product packaging in e-commerce fulfillment.

## Circularity Rate %

- **Name:** Packaging Circularity Rate
- **Definition:** The percentage of packaging material weight that is derived from recycled, compostable, or certified biodegradable streams.
- **Formula:** Circularity Rate % = (Circular Packaging Weight) / (Total Packaging Weight) x 100
- **Direction:** higher-is-better
- **Authoritative Source ID:** `ESG-R01`
- **Typical Refresh Cadence:** Monthly
- **Glossary cross-reference:** Packaging Circularity

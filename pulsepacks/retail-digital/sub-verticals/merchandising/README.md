# Merchandising Sub-Vertical

Focuses on gross margin efficiency, inventory productivity, sell-through velocity, and markdowns.

## Focus Areas
- **Profitability:** Analyzing category GMROI to optimize pricing and promotion strategies.
- **Stock Rotation:** Auditing sell-through rates to prevent slow-moving stock from tying up working capital.
- **Inventory Turn:** Directing supply re-orders dynamically to balance availability against inventory carrying cost.

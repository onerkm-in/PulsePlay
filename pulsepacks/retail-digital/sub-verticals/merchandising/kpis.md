# Retail Merchandising KPIs

Detailed definitions and formulas for core retail merchandising metrics.

## KPIs

### Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI)
- **Label:** GMROI Ratio
- **Formula:** `GMROI = (Gross Margin $) / (Average Inventory Cost)`
- **Direction:** `higher-is-better`
- **Thresholds:** Good: `>= 2.5`, Watch: `1.5 - 2.49`, Risk: `< 1.5`
- **Source:** `RET-001`
- **Description:** Assesses the capital efficiency of stock categories.

### Sell-Through Rate
- **Label:** Sell-Through %
- **Formula:** `Sell-Through % = (Units Sold) / (Starting Inventory Units) x 100`
- **Direction:** `higher-is-better`
- **Source:** `RET-001`
- **Description:** The speed at which inventory is cleared.

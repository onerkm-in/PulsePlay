# BI-AI Integration Guide — Digital Marketing

Bridges digital marketing campaigns and user acquisition metrics to analytics systems.

## Schema Expectations
- **Campaign Spend Table:** Expected fields `campaign_id`, `ad_platform`, `spend_amount`, `clicks`, `impressions`, `date`.
- **User Cohort Attribution Table:** Expected fields `customer_id`, `acquisition_source`, `first_purchase_date`, `first_purchase_amount`.

## Visualization Layout
- Use bar charts to compare ROAS across ad platforms (Google, Meta, TikTok).
- Use cohort grids/heatmaps to show LTV progression over a 12-month span.

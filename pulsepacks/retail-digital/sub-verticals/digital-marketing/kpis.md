# Digital Marketing KPIs

Detailed definitions and formulas for core growth marketing metrics.

## KPIs

### Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS)
- **Label:** ROAS
- **Formula:** `ROAS = (Ad-driven Revenue) / (Ad Spend)`
- **Direction:** `higher-is-better`
- **Thresholds:** Good: `>= 4.0`, Watch: `2.5 - 3.99`, Risk: `< 2.5`
- **Source:** `RET-002`
- **Description:** Returns the revenue multiple generated per dollar of marketing spend.

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Label:** CAC
- **Formula:** `CAC = (Total Marketing Cost) / (Total Acquired Customers)`
- **Direction:** `lower-is-better`
- **Source:** `RET-002`
- **Description:** The cost to acquire a new customer segment.

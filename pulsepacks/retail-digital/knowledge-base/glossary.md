# E-Commerce & Retail Glossary

> **Owner:** Retail Analytics & S&OP Committee
> **Author:** Retail Domain Architect
> **Last reviewed:** 2026-05-23
> **Source register:** `knowledge-base/references.md`
> **Confidence:** reviewed

- **GMROI (Gross Margin Return on Investment)** — A retail merchandising metric that measures the profitability of inventory. Calculated as gross margin dollars divided by the average inventory cost. Source: `RET-001`.
- **Sell-Through Rate** — The percentage of received inventory sold during a specific time period relative to the starting inventory. Source: `RET-001`.
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)** — The total growth marketing spend allocated to acquire a single customer. Source: `RET-002`.
- **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)** — Gross ad-driven revenue divided by the corresponding ad campaign spend. Source: `RET-002`.
- **Packaging Circularity** — The proportion of packaging materials distributed with customer shipments that are biodegradable, compostable, or fully recyclable. Source: `ESG-R01`.

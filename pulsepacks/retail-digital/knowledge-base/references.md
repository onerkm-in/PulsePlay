# E-Commerce & Retail Source Register

This register catalogues standard bibliography references used in E-Commerce and Digital Retail presets.

## References

### `RET-001`
- **Publisher/Body:** National Retail Federation (NRF)
- **Title:** Retail Merchandising Mathematics Standard Guide
- **URL:** https://nrf.com/resources/retail-library
- **Scope:** Merchandising, GMROI, and Inventory Velocity calculations
- **Credibility Tier:** `tier-1-standard`

### `RET-002`
- **Publisher/Body:** Google Developers / GA4
- **Title:** Google Analytics 4 Enhanced Ecommerce Schema
- **URL:** https://developers.google.com/analytics/devguides/collection/ga4/reference/events
- **Scope:** Conversion funnels, attribution, and ROAS parameters
- **Credibility Tier:** `tier-2-official-product`

### `ESG-R01`
- **Publisher/Body:** Greenhouse Gas Protocol
- **Title:** Corporate Value Chain (Scope 3) Standard
- **URL:** https://ghgprotocol.org/standards/scope-3-standard
- **Scope:** Carbon intensity per delivery and packaging footprint
- **Credibility Tier:** `tier-1-standard`

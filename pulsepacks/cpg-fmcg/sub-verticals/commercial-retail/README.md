# Commercial / Retail

The commercial seat covers revenue growth management (RGM), trade promotion, customer business planning, retail execution, digital shelf, and category management. In CPG/FMCG, this is where margin recovery and growth strategy meet retailer collaboration.

## What this sub-vertical covers

- **Revenue Growth Management (RGM)**: pricing, volume / mix, trade investment, promo ROI, pack architecture, elasticity.
- **Trade promotion**: planning, execution, accrual, settlement, post-event analysis, leakage detection.
- **Customer / retailer business planning**: JBP, scorecards, growth and margin commitments.
- **Retail execution**: shelf availability, planogram compliance, out-of-stock detection, phantom inventory.
- **Digital shelf**: search rank, content quality, ratings, reviews, price parity, competitor moves.
- **Omnichannel attribution**: store, marketplace, quick-commerce, social commerce, DTC, last-mile.
- **Retail media / shopper marketing**: incrementality, halo, cannibalization, diminishing returns.

## Why a CPG team uses this

- "Promo P on customer X underperformed expected lift by 22%. What drove it (price gap, availability, competing promo, weather)?"
- "Category C is losing share at retailer R. Is it assortment, distribution, price gap, or content quality on .com?"
- "JBP commitments for FY are at risk on volume. Which levers (price, mix, distribution, promo) close the gap with the lowest margin sacrifice?"
- "Trade spend accrual is over-committed by 8%. Where is the leakage (off-invoice, MDF, scan-back, post-event)?"
- "Digital shelf health for our top 50 SKUs at retailer R — ranking, content score, ratings trajectory, price parity."

## Typical data sources

- **TPM**: SAP TPM, Salesforce, Exceedra, Vistex, custom RGM/TPM engines.
- **Syndicated retail measurement**: NielsenIQ, Circana, Kantar, IRI heritage datasets, retailer-specific panels.
- **Retailer data**: Walmart Retail Link, Target Partners Online, Kroger Stratum / 84.51, Tesco Connect, retailer-specific portals.
- **Digital shelf analytics**: Profitero, Salsify, Stackline, DataWeave, Edge by Ascential.
- **CDP / loyalty**: Salesforce Customer 360, Adobe Real-Time CDP, Treasure Data, retailer-loyalty platforms.
- **CRM**: Salesforce.
- **Internal**: net-revenue, gross-to-net, P&L by customer / category, customer master.

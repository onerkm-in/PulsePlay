# Commercial / Retail — KPIs

## Net Revenue

- **Definition.** Gross sales minus revenue deductions (trade discounts, returns, allowances).
- **Formula.** Net revenue = gross sales - trade - returns - allowances.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard CPG finance / commercial KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Daily.

## Gross-to-Net (GtN) Ratio

- **Definition.** Ratio of net revenue to gross revenue; an inverse measure of trade-investment intensity.
- **Formula.** GtN = (net revenue) / (gross revenue).
- **Direction.** Higher is generally better, but trade investment is often necessary for volume / share, so target band.
- **Source / standard.** Standard CPG finance KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Promo ROI

- **Definition.** Incremental margin generated by a promotion divided by promotional spend.
- **Formula.** Promo ROI = (incremental margin) / (promo spend). Incremental margin requires a baseline counterfactual.
- **Direction.** Higher is better; >1.0 indicates the promo more than paid for itself in margin.
- **Source / standard.** Standard marketing-effectiveness practice.
- **Refresh cadence.** Per-event post-mortem within 30 days of event close.

## Distribution / All-Commodity Volume (ACV)

- **Definition.** Percentage of total category-relevant retail volume in stores carrying the SKU. Often expressed as %ACV.
- **Formula.** %ACV = (ACV of stores carrying SKU) / (total category ACV) x 100.
- **Direction.** Higher is better (within strategic assortment).
- **Source / standard.** Standard NielsenIQ / Circana retail measurement.
- **Refresh cadence.** Weekly to monthly depending on syndicated feed.

## Share of Shelf / Share of Search

- **Definition.** Share of shelf is the company's products' share of total facings on the planogram. Share of search is the company's share of search rank prominence on retailer.com or marketplace search results.
- **Formula.**
  - Share of shelf % = (company facings) / (total category facings) x 100
  - Share of search % = visibility-weighted ranking share
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard retail-merchandising / digital-shelf practice.
- **Refresh cadence.** Quarterly (shelf); daily (digital-shelf-analytics tools).

## Lift % (promotion)

- **Definition.** Sales lift caused by the promotion above baseline.
- **Formula.** Lift % = ((actual sales during promo) - (baseline sales)) / (baseline sales) x 100.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard promo-analytics practice.
- **Refresh cadence.** Per-event.

## Forecast Accuracy (commercial)

- **Definition.** Accuracy of commercial forecast (volume, net revenue) at customer-category-month granularity. Distinct from supply chain forecast accuracy, which is at SKU-location-week granularity.
- **Formula.** 1 - WMAPE at appropriate granularity.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard commercial-finance practice.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Customer / Account P&L Margin

- **Definition.** Gross margin or contribution margin attributable to a specific customer or account, after trade and cost-to-serve.
- **Formula.** Customer margin = (customer net revenue) - (allocated COGS) - (trade) - (cost-to-serve attribution).
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard managerial-accounting practice; specific allocation rules vary.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Trade Spend % of Net Revenue

- **Definition.** Trade investment as a percentage of net revenue, sliced by customer and mechanic.
- **Formula.** Trade spend % = (trade spend) / (net revenue) x 100.
- **Direction.** Target band; differs by category, customer, and channel maturity.
- **Source / standard.** Standard CPG commercial KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Out-of-Stock %

- **Definition.** Percentage of store-SKU facings that should have product on shelf but do not.
- **Formula.** Out-of-stock % = (store-SKU instances flagged out-of-stock) / (total store-SKU facings checked) x 100.
- **Direction.** Lower is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard retail-execution KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Daily where retailer feeds support; otherwise weekly.

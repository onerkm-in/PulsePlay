# HR

The HR seat in a CPG/FMCG enterprise covers workforce planning across plants, DCs, sales territories, and corporate functions; skills development for both frontline (operators, drivers, sales reps) and specialist roles (planners, RGM analysts, data and AI roles); safety and frontline experience; and the organisation's response to AI-augmentation in white-collar work.

## What this sub-vertical covers

- **Workforce planning** by plant, DC, sales territory, shift, skill, absenteeism, and demand seasonality.
- **Skills graph and learning paths** for supply chain planners, line operators, maintenance, sales, category, data, and AI roles.
- **Safety and fatigue analytics** with cross-vertical link to manufacturing.
- **Recruiting intelligence** for frontline and specialist roles.
- **Internal talent marketplace** for project-based transformation work.
- **AI-augmentation programs**: tracking where AI helps teams make better decisions, not just where it removes tasks (the "Human-Amplified Workbench" pattern).

## Why a CPG team uses this

- "Plant P is short 14 trained operators on the night shift. Where do we recruit, redeploy, or accept overtime?"
- "Recordable safety incidents on line 7 have spiked. Is it staffing, training, equipment, or shift pattern?"
- "Sales territory T attrition is up 18%. Pay band, manager span, geography, or specific manager?"
- "We are committing to AI-literacy training across planning. What does the skill-gap map look like and where do we start?"

## Typical data sources

- **HRIS**: Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, Oracle HCM Cloud.
- **Time and attendance / shift**: Kronos / UKG, ADP, plant-native shift systems.
- **Safety**: SAP EHS, Intelex, Cority, Velocity EHS, internal incident logs.
- **Learning**: Workday Learning, SuccessFactors LMS, Cornerstone, Degreed, LinkedIn Learning, internal academies.
- **Recruiting**: Workday Recruiting, SmartRecruiters, Greenhouse, iCIMS.
- **Survey / engagement**: Glint, Qualtrics EX, Peakon, Culture Amp.

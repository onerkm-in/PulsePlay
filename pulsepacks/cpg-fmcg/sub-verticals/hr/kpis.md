# HR — KPIs

## Voluntary Attrition Rate

- **Definition.** Percentage of employees who voluntarily leave the organisation in a period.
- **Formula.** Voluntary attrition % = (voluntary leavers) / (average headcount) x 100, annualised.
- **Direction.** Lower is generally better; some level of voluntary turnover is healthy.
- **Source / standard.** Standard HR KPI; SHRM / IHRIM common practice.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Regretted Attrition

- **Definition.** Voluntary attrition restricted to performers the org wanted to retain.
- **Formula.** Regretted attrition % = (regretted voluntary leavers) / (average headcount) x 100. Specific definition of "regretted" is org-defined (typically based on performance rating bands).
- **Direction.** Lower is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard people-analytics KPI; org-defined criteria.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Time to Fill

- **Definition.** Days from requisition open to offer accepted, by role family.
- **Formula.** Time to fill = (offer accepted date) - (requisition opened date), averaged.
- **Direction.** Lower is better, balanced against quality of hire.
- **Source / standard.** Standard recruiting KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Internal Mobility Rate

- **Definition.** Percentage of role moves filled internally vs externally.
- **Formula.** Internal mobility % = (internal hires) / (total hires) x 100.
- **Direction.** Higher is generally better (signal of development and career-pathing).
- **Source / standard.** Standard people-analytics KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Quarterly.

## Recordable Safety Incident Rate (TRIR / TRIFR)

- **Definition.** See [Manufacturing KPIs](../manufacturing/kpis.md). Owned jointly by HR / EHS and manufacturing.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Engagement Score

- **Definition.** Composite score from periodic engagement surveys; specific composite varies by survey vendor.
- **Formula.** Definition-only; specific composites use Glint, Qualtrics EX, Peakon, Culture Amp methodologies.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Survey-vendor-specific.
- **Refresh cadence.** Quarterly to annual.

## Diversity Representation

- **Definition.** Representation of demographic groups at total / leadership / pipeline levels, vs internal targets and external benchmarks.
- **Formula.** Representation % = (employees in group) / (total employees in scope) x 100, sliced by level and function.
- **Direction.** Org-defined targets; aligns with GRI 405 (Diversity and Equal Opportunity) for sustainability disclosure.
- **Source / standard.** GRI 405. https://www.globalreporting.org/standards/
- **Refresh cadence.** Quarterly.

## Cost per Hire

- **Definition.** Total cost (recruiter time, advertising, agency fees, signing bonuses) per filled requisition.
- **Formula.** Cost per hire = (total recruiting costs) / (filled requisitions).
- **Direction.** Lower is better, balanced against quality of hire.
- **Source / standard.** Standard recruiting KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Quarterly.

## Training Hours / Spend per Employee

- **Definition.** Average training hours and spend per employee per period.
- **Formula.** Training intensity = (total training hours or spend) / (average headcount).
- **Direction.** Target band; aligns with GRI 404 (Training and Education) for sustainability disclosure.
- **Source / standard.** GRI 404.
- **Refresh cadence.** Quarterly.

## Span of Control

- **Definition.** Average number of direct reports per manager.
- **Formula.** Span = (managed employees) / (managers).
- **Direction.** Target band; too narrow drives overhead, too wide drives manager burden.
- **Source / standard.** Standard org-design KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Quarterly.

# Procurement

The procurement seat covers sourcing, supplier risk, contract management, commodity exposure, and supplier-facing sustainability scorecards. In CPG, procurement increasingly carries P&L exposure (ingredient inflation, packaging volatility) and ESG exposure (Scope 3 supplier emissions).

## What this sub-vertical covers

- **Strategic sourcing**: category strategy, supplier rationalisation, RFx orchestration.
- **Operational procurement**: PO management, three-way matching, on-time delivery from suppliers.
- **Contract intelligence**: obligations, renewals, rebates, penalties, force-majeure, price-index triggers.
- **Commodity exposure**: price trends, hedging, FX, supplier-side index pass-through clauses.
- **Supplier risk**: financial health, geopolitical, concentration, cyber, quality, sustainability, tier 2/3/4 dependencies.
- **Sustainable procurement**: Scope 3 supplier emissions, supplier ESG scorecards, evidence collection for CSRD.

The deeper supplier 360 view (commercial + operational + financial + risk + ESG) lives in the dedicated [Vendor Management](../vendor-management/README.md) sub-vertical, which this one cross-references heavily.

## Why a CPG team uses this

- "Sugar futures moved 7% — what is our forward exposure and which suppliers carry pass-through clauses?"
- "Supplier S has a quality incident streak. Is it isolated, or do we have a tier 2 dependency masked behind it?"
- "Renewal cycle for category C starts in 90 days. Which incumbents are renegotiation candidates and which are sole-source risks?"
- "We need a 3-supplier shortlist for an aluminium-can RFx. Compliance, sustainability, financial-health filters applied."
- "What is our Scope 3 category-1 emissions trajectory by supplier, and where is our largest reduction lever?"

## Typical data sources

- **Source-to-pay platforms**: SAP Ariba, Coupa, Ivalua, Jaggaer, GEP, Zycus, Kissflow.
- **ERP procurement modules**: SAP MM, Oracle Procurement, Microsoft Dynamics SCM.
- **Contract lifecycle management**: Icertis, Agiloft, Ironclad, DocuSign CLM.
- **Supplier risk and intelligence**: D&B, Bloomberg, Reuters, RapidRatings, Resilinc, EcoVadis, Sayari, Interos, Sphera.
- **Commodity data**: CRB, S&P Global Commodity Insights (Platts), Argus, ICIS, Mintec.
- **Internal**: spend cube, vendor master, AP data, quality systems (deviations and complaints).

## Notable industry context

The Gartner research from July 2025 explicitly noted that GenAI for procurement has entered the **trough of disillusionment**, with fragmented data and integration complexity limiting ROI. See https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2025-07-30-gartner-says-generative-ai-for-procurement-has-entered-the-trough-of-disillusionment. Pack design recommendation: keep procurement AI use cases narrow and focused on RFx draft, supplier risk surfacing, contract obligation lookup, and commodity narrative. Avoid open-ended "make procurement autonomous" framing.

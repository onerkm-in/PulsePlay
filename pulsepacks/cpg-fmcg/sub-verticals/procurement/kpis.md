# Procurement — KPIs

## Spend Under Management

- **Definition.** Percentage of total addressable spend that is being actively managed (sourced, contracted, and tracked) by procurement.
- **Formula.** Spend under management % = (managed spend) / (total addressable spend) x 100.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard procurement KPI; tracked by The Hackett Group benchmarks.
- **Refresh cadence.** Quarterly.

## Realised Savings vs. Plan

- **Definition.** Realised savings (cost avoidance + cost reduction) tracked against the procurement annual plan.
- **Formula.** Realised savings % = (realised savings) / (planned savings) x 100.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard procurement KPI; definitions vary on whether avoidance and one-time savings count.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Supplier On-Time Delivery (OTD)

- **Definition.** Percentage of supplier deliveries received on or before the agreed delivery date.
- **Formula.** Supplier OTD % = (deliveries on or before agreed date) / (total deliveries) x 100, sliced by supplier and category.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard supply / procurement KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Daily for operational view; weekly for supplier scorecards.

## Supplier Quality (PPM Defects or Acceptance Rate)

- **Definition.** Defect rate of incoming materials, expressed as parts-per-million defective or acceptance rate.
- **Formula.** PPM defects = (defective units) / (total units received) x 1,000,000.
- **Direction.** Lower is better (PPM); higher is better (acceptance %).
- **Source / standard.** Standard supplier quality KPI; QM module of the ERP.
- **Refresh cadence.** Weekly.

## Contract Coverage

- **Definition.** Percentage of spend covered by an active contract.
- **Formula.** Contract coverage % = (contracted spend) / (total spend) x 100.
- **Direction.** Higher is better; high contract coverage is a precondition for trustable Scope 3 spend-based emissions calculations.
- **Source / standard.** Standard procurement governance KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Maverick Spend

- **Definition.** Spend that bypassed the contracted supplier or PR/PO process.
- **Formula.** Maverick spend % = (off-contract or unauthorised spend) / (total spend) x 100.
- **Direction.** Lower is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard procurement governance KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Days Payable Outstanding (DPO)

- **Definition.** Average days a company takes to pay its suppliers.
- **Formula.** DPO = (accounts payable) / (cost of goods sold per day).
- **Direction.** Higher DPO frees working capital but may damage supplier relationships and inflate prices via supplier financing costs. Target band.
- **Source / standard.** Standard finance KPI; falls between procurement and finance.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Commodity Exposure (forward-looking)

- **Definition.** Net exposure (long / short / hedged) by commodity over a forward horizon.
- **Formula.** Definition-only at KPI level; built up from contracted volumes, hedge positions, and forward demand requirements.
- **Direction.** Target band (defined by treasury and risk policy).
- **Source / standard.** Standard treasury / procurement practice.
- **Refresh cadence.** Daily for active commodities; weekly for tracked.

## Supplier Concentration Risk

- **Definition.** Share of category spend held by the top 1, top 3, top 5 suppliers.
- **Formula.** Top-N concentration = (spend with top N suppliers) / (total category spend).
- **Direction.** Lower is generally better for resilience; trade-off against scale economics.
- **Source / standard.** Standard procurement risk KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Quarterly.

## Supplier ESG Score (Scope 3 contribution)

- **Definition.** Composite score reflecting supplier-reported environmental, social, and governance performance, plus the supplier's contribution to the company's Scope 3 emissions.
- **Formula.** Definition-only; specific composites use EcoVadis ratings, CDP disclosure scores, SBTi alignment, and the company's spend-based or supplier-specific Scope 3 attribution.
- **Direction.** Higher ESG score is better; lower attributed Scope 3 is better.
- **Source / standard.** GHG Protocol Corporate Value Chain (Scope 3) Standard for the emissions side; EcoVadis / CDP / SBTi for the disclosure side.
- **Refresh cadence.** Annual (with quarterly refresh as supplier disclosures arrive).

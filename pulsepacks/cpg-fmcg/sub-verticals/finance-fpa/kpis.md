# Finance / FP&A — KPIs

## Net Revenue

- **Definition.** See [Commercial / Retail KPIs](../commercial-retail/kpis.md). Repeated here because it is the headline finance KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Daily.

## Gross Margin

- **Definition.** Net revenue minus cost of goods sold (COGS), expressed as a percentage of net revenue.
- **Formula.** Gross margin % = ((net revenue) - (COGS)) / (net revenue) x 100.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard finance KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Daily for actuals; monthly for plan-vs-actual variance reporting.

## EBITDA / EBITDA Margin

- **Definition.** Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortisation. EBITDA margin = EBITDA / net revenue.
- **Formula.** EBITDA = operating income + depreciation + amortisation. EBITDA margin % = EBITDA / net revenue x 100.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard finance KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)

- **Definition.** Days sales outstanding (DSO) + days inventory on hand (DIO) - days payable outstanding (DPO). Measures how long working capital is tied up.
- **Formula.** CCC = DSO + DIO - DPO.
- **Direction.** Lower is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard finance / treasury KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)

- **Definition.** Average days a company takes to collect on its receivables.
- **Formula.** DSO = (accounts receivable) / (net credit sales per day).
- **Direction.** Lower is better, with attention to customer-relationship implications.
- **Source / standard.** Standard finance KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Forecast Accuracy (financial)

- **Definition.** Accuracy of the financial forecast at the appropriate granularity (entity / segment / region).
- **Formula.** 1 - WMAPE; or absolute variance to plan.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard FP&A KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Trade Spend Accrual Accuracy

- **Definition.** Variance between forward-recognised trade-spend accruals and final settled trade-spend payouts.
- **Formula.** Accrual accuracy % = 1 - |accrual - settlement| / settlement.
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard CPG finance / commercial KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Deduction Leakage

- **Definition.** Deductions accepted (or written off) that should have been disputed and recovered.
- **Formula.** Definition-only at KPI level; specific calculations vary by deduction-management practice.
- **Direction.** Lower is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard CPG receivables / commercial KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Operating Cash Flow

- **Definition.** Cash generated from operating activities.
- **Formula.** Operating cash flow per the cash-flow statement (indirect or direct method).
- **Direction.** Higher is better.
- **Source / standard.** Standard finance KPI; reported per IFRS / US GAAP standards.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

## Capex Plan Adherence

- **Definition.** Actual capital expenditure vs planned, by project and timing.
- **Formula.** Capex adherence % = (actual capex) / (planned capex) x 100.
- **Direction.** Target band (under-spend can indicate execution issues; over-spend indicates governance issues).
- **Source / standard.** Standard finance / treasury KPI.
- **Refresh cadence.** Monthly.

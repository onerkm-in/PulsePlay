Secure embedding by construction

Every embed credential — Power BI embed tokens, Tableau trusted tickets, Qlik OAuth, Looker signed URLs — is minted server-side by the proxy. Nothing sensitive ever reaches the browser, and each vendor runs in the narrowest sandbox it needs.
